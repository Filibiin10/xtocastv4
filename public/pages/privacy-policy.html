Privacy Policy

1. Personal Information:

We may collect personal information that you provide to us when you register, use our services, or communicate with us. This may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Payment information

2. Usage Data:

We collect information about how you interact with our platform, such as:

  • IP address

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on Xtocast. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us understand your preferences and usage patterns.

  • Protect against fraudulent or unauthorized activity
